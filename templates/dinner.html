Dinner

Dinner usually refers to the most significant and important meal of the day, which can be the noon or the evening meal.

However, the term "dinner" can have many different meanings depending on the culture; it may mean a meal of any size eaten at any time of day.

Did you know? The meaning as the evening meal, generally the largest of the day, is becoming standard in many parts of the English-speaking world.
