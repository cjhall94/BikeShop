Lunch

Lunch is a midday meal, of varying size depending on the culture.

The origin of the words lunch and luncheon relate to a small meal originally eaten at any time of the day or night, but during the 20th century gradually focused toward a small or mid-sized meal eaten at midday.

Did you know? Lunch is the second meal of the day after breakfest!
