Breakfest

Breakfest is the first meal taken after rising from a night's sleep, most often eaten in the early morning before undertaking the day's work.

Among English speakers, "breakfest" can be used to refer to this meal composed of traditional breakfest foods(such as eggs, porridge and sausage) served at any time of day.

Did you know? The word "breakfest" literally refers to breaking the fasting period of the prior night.
